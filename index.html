Each week, I create a curriculum centered around a different theme, allowing the children to explore diverse artistic concepts and techniques. I provide step-by-step art demonstrations that guide the kids through the creative process, ensuring they feel confident as they experiment with their artistic skills. Additionally, I incorporate fun games during their breaks, promoting a joyful and energetic environment where learning and play coexist harmoniously. In addition to my teaching responsibilities, I serve as the Public Relations Chair for UCR's Katipunan, a Pilipino student-led organization on campus. In this role, I design eye-catching graphics for every event throughout the academic year. My responsibilities include coordinating with my Co-Chair to brainstorm innovative ideas and plan future graphics that resonate with our community's interests. To enhance event visibility, I actively market and post about upcoming events on social media, aiming to increase turnout and excitement. Collaborating with other board members is crucial; I reach out to gain insight into their visions for events, ensuring that these ideas are effectively reflected in the promotional materials I create. Furthermore, I am a dedicated member of the Marketing and Design team, where I design infographics and merchandise that capture the essence of our events. My commitment to creating a visually appealing online presence is essential in fostering engagement within our community. Alongside my academic and extracurricular involvement, I participated in the Philippine American Culture Night (PACN) internship. During this experience, I shadowed dance coaches, directors, and coordinators, gaining valuable insights into their roles and contributions to the performances. Working closely with my peers, we efficiently divided responsibilities to accomplish our goals and ensure the success of the PACN.
